What is a 'Girl Month'?

Since his first girl week in August 2020, F1NN5TER has been doing Girl Month donation goals. Every time donators reach the goal, another month is added - at the time of writing, we've just hit the Girl Year! Originally, F1nn had to adopt the 'Rose Evergreen' persona, only using she/her pronouns, wearing heels regularly, etc.

As things have progressed, F1nn has "scammed" (according to some) his way out of the heels requirement and the 'Rose' persona has become more of a meme. No matter what, if we keep meeting the girl month goals are reached, we'll keep F1nn in girly clothes for the rest of time.
